🃏 How to Play UNO

Setup

Each player is dealt 7 cards. The remaining cards form the draw pile. One card is flipped to start the discard pile.

On Your Turn

Play a card from your hand that matches the top discard card by color, number, or symbol. If you can't play, draw one card — you may then play it if it matches, or pass.

Special Cards

  • Skip (⊘) — The next player loses their turn.
  • Reverse (↺) — Reverses play direction. With 2 players, acts like Skip.
  • Draw Two (+2) — Next player draws 2 cards and loses their turn.
  • Wild (★) — Play anytime. You choose the new active color.
  • Wild Draw Four (+4) — Play only if you have no cards matching the current color. You choose the new color, and the next player draws 4 cards and loses their turn.

UNO!

When you play down to 1 card, click the UNO! button immediately. If another player catches you before you do, you draw 2 penalty cards.

House Rules

  • Stack Draw Cards — When a +2 is played, the next player can play another +2 to pass it on. The first player who can't stack must draw the accumulated total.
  • Draw Until Match — Instead of drawing one card, you keep drawing until you get a card you can play.
  • Force Play — You cannot draw if you have a playable card in your hand.
  • Seven-O Rules — Playing a 7 lets you swap hands with any other player. Playing a 0 rotates everyone's hands in the direction of play.

Winning

The first player to play all of their cards wins the round!
